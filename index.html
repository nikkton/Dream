Who she is?

She is not just a person I met, she is the chapter that rewrote my entire story. She is the kind of girl who can make a room feel alive simply by walking into it. She talks too much when she is excited and goes completely silent when she is upset, and both versions of her are beautiful. She has this strange ability to make me laugh even when I have no reason to. She gets annoyed at small things, but her anger never lasts longer than her love. She is the kind of person who will fight with me over silly reasons and still be the first one to check if I had my dinner. She is sensitive, sometimes more than the world deserves, but that is what makes her heart rare. She has a way of turning even the most ordinary evening into a memory worth keeping. She is dramatic in ways that only make her more lovable. She forgets things easily but never forgets how to make me feel special. She is messy in her habits yet organized in her emotions. She can be stubborn, but it is the kind of stubbornness that shows she cares deeply. She is silly in the purest way, the kind of silly that makes life lighter.
